Software

  • Archeofrag: R package implementing a method to evaluate the consistency of archaeological stratigraphy from the topology of the refitting relationships between fragments. Access to the code, access to the Shiny application.

  • Virtual Poeymaü: online visualisation of the archaeological remains collected during the excavation of the Poeymaü cave (Pyrenees) by Georges Laplace during the 1950s. It was developped in the context of the PAVO project (“Early Prehistory of the Ossau Valley”).

  • MAP Syntax Diagram: a Shiny application for the analysis divine onomastic sequences from the Old World. It was developped in the framework of the “Mapping Ancient Polytheims” project. Access to the application; link to the documentation.
